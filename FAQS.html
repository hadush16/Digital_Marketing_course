Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

1. When does DV-2027 registration start?

The DV-2027 Diversity Visa program is expected to open in early October 2025 and run until early November 2025. Always confirm the exact start and end dates on the official U.S. Department of State website: dvprogram.state.gov.

2. How can I pay the DV-2027 entry fee?

The official DV entry fee is USD 1. Digital SunriseAds helps applicants handle this small payment securely through local methods such as Bank transfer, TeleBirr, or M-Pesa. You’ll receive confirmation and a record of your payment after submission.
