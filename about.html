Payment Process
  1. Browse and select your desired course from the Home or Service page.
  2. Click "Enroll Now" to view course details, price, and any available discounts.
  3. Click "Enroll Now" again to proceed to the sign up/sign in page.
  4. After signing up or signing in, you will be redirected to the payment/contact form.
  5. Fill in your contact details and submit the form. Payment instructions will be sent to your email or displayed on the screen.
  6. Once payment is confirmed, you will receive access to your selected course and all related materials.
