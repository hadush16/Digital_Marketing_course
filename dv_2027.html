DV-2027 — Instructions & USD 1 Payment Assistance

Official site: dvprogram.state.gov

Quick facts

Expected entry window: early Oct 2025 → early Nov 2025 (likely ~Nov 5). Results: typically announced in May following year. Confirm exact dates on the official DV Program website.

Official DV entry fee is $1. Digital SunriseAds provides advisory help and will assist with the USD 1 entry payment on client request.

What you must do (steps)

  1. Provide your details using the contact form on the right (full name, email, country of birth).
  2. Send a recent passport-style photo that meets DV specs (we'll validate it).
  3. Send the equivalent payment for $1 entry handling/payment via the secure payment link/instructions we provide (Bank or wallet).

What Digital SunriseAds will do (steps)

  1. Fill the DV entry form based on the details you provided (we will share the completed draft with you for confirmation).
  2. Check and confirm photo eligibility against DV requirements; request a replacement if needed.
  3. Process the USD 1 entry handling payment through the secure payment channel.
  4. Return payment confirmation and a summary of the submitted entry so you can keep records and check status on the official site dvprogram.state.gov.

Notes

  • We will never ask for your Entrant Status confirmation number. Keep that number safe — you need it to check results.
  • All payments are processed via secure providers.
Use Entrant Status Check on dvprogram.state.gov to view results.
Be cautious of scams.
